Common Questions

How does syncing work?
Clear Ledger syncs automatically via iCloud. Just make sure you're signed in to the same iCloud account on all your devices. It may take a moment for new entries to appear on other devices.

Can I export my data?
Yes. Clear Ledger supports exporting your journal, trial balance, balance sheet, and profit & loss reports as PDF or CSV files.

How do I delete a transaction?
Go to the Transactions tab, swipe left on the entry you want to remove, and tap Delete.

Is my data private?
Absolutely. Your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We don't collect any data. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
